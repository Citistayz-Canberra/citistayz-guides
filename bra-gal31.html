🏢 Getting to Your Apartment

The Gallery Apartments — Unit 31, Level 5

🔑
You should have already collected your keys from 23 Lonsdale Street, Braddon before going to the property. If you don't have your keys yet, tap here for key collection instructions or call us on 0493 173 122.
Step 1
Enter the Building
Head to The Gallery entrance on Mort Street. Walk through the entrance archway and turn left to reach the foyer doors as shown by the arrow.
Step 2
Your Keys & Remote
Your key set includes a button remote, apartment key and fob. The remote acts as your access swipe for all lifts and external doors — swipe it on the black box with the blue light for entry.
Step 3
Take the Lift to Level 5
Head to the lift shown below. Swipe your remote on the black box with the blue light inside the lift then press Level 5 (circled in the photo).
Step 4
Find Apartment 31
Exit the lift on Level 5 and turn right. Walk all the way to the end of the corridor — Apartment 31 is the last door. Use your apartment key to enter.
Step 1
Car Park Entry
Enter the car park on the left side of the 40 Mort St sign as shown by the arrow. Take the right hand side garage door — there are two garage doors, make sure you use the right one.
Step 2
Open the Garage Door
Press the button on your remote to open the garage door. Important: you need to be quite close to the door for the remote button to work. Press it when you are right in front of the door.
Step 3
Drive Down to Level B1 — Bay 31
Go down 1 ramp to Level B1. Your parking space is Bay 31, located to the left of the lift.
Step 4
Take the Lift to Level 5
The lift is located just next to your parking bay. Swipe your remote on the black box with the blue light and select Level 5.
Step 5
Find Apartment 31
Exit the lift on Level 5 and turn right. Walk all the way to the end of the corridor — Apartment 31 is the last door. Use your apartment key to enter.
