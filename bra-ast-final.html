🏢 Getting to Your Apartment

The Astin Apartments — Unit 17, Level 2

🔑
Keys Required Before Proceeding
You should have already collected your keys from 23 Lonsdale Street, Braddon before going to the property. If you don't have your keys yet, please collect them first or call us on 0493 173 122.
Step 1
Enter the Building
Walk to the foyer doors shown below. Swipe your blue key fob on the black box (indicated by the arrow) to open the doors.
Step 2
Take the Lift to Level 2
Use any of the 3 lifts. Inside the lift, swipe your blue key fob on the black box then press Level 2 (shown by the arrow).
Step 3
Find Apartment 17
Turn right out of the lift and walk all the way to the end of the corridor. Apartment 17 is the last door on your left.
Step 1
Car Park Entry & First Door
Drive into the car park entry on the left side of the building (arrow shows entry). Swipe your blue key fob on the reader to open the first door.
Step 2
Open the Second Gate
Drive through and swipe your blue key fob on the reader shown on the right to open the second gate and access the basement.
Step 3
Drive Down to Level B3 — Bay 17
Drive all the way down the ramps to the very bottom — Level B3. Your space is Bay 17, to the right of the concrete pillar (arrows show the way).
Step 4
Take the Lift Up to Level 2
The lift is on the right hand side of the car park. Swipe your blue key fob and select Level 2.
Step 5
Find Apartment 17
Turn right out of the lift and walk all the way to the end of the corridor. Apartment 17 is the last door on your left.
⚠️ Having trouble? Call us immediately on 0493 173 122. We respond within minutes.
