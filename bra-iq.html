🏢 Getting to Your Apartment

Braddon IQ — Unit 106, Level 1

🔑
You should have already collected your keys from 23 Lonsdale Street, Braddon before going to the property. If you don't have your keys yet, tap here for key collection instructions or call us on 0493 173 122.
Step 1
Enter the Building
Walk to the foyer doors at 102 Northbourne Avenue. Use your white key card on the scanner located to the right opposite the letterboxes to open the foyer doors.
Step 2
Take the Lift to Level 1
Take the lift to Level 1 (highlighted in the photo). Swipe your white key card on the reader inside the lift then press Level 1.
Step 3
Find Apartment 106
Exit the lift on Level 1 and follow the signs to Apartment 106. Look for the Citi Stayz door mat out the front. Enjoy your stay!
