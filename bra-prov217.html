🏢 Getting to Your Apartment

Provenance — Unit 217, Level 2

🔑
Keys Required Before Proceeding
You should have already collected your keys from 23 Lonsdale Street, Braddon before going to the property. If you don't have your keys yet, tap here for key collection instructions or call us on 0493 173 122.
Your Keys
Black Remote & Red Key
Your key set includes a black remote (opens carpark entry doors and also acts as your lift access) and a red physical key (opens the apartment door). To activate the lift, hold the remote above the button panel.
Step 1
Enter the Building
Make your way to Provenance at 43 Currong Street North, Braddon. Use your remote to access the building entrance and proceed to the lifts.
Step 2
Take the Lift to Level 2
Inside the lift, hold the remote above the button panel to activate it, then press Level 2 (circled in gold).
Step 3
Find Apartment 217
Exit the lift on Level 2 and head straight ahead to the left at the end of the corridor. Apartment 217 is at the far end.
Step 1
Drive to 2 Batman Street
The carpark entry is at the back of the building — go to 2 Batman Street, Braddon (not the main building address). Turn left into Batman Street — the entry is on your left.
Step 2
Open the Entry & Take the First Right
Use your remote to open the left-hand door. Drive down the ramp and take the first right.
Step 3
Take the Second Right
Continue and take the second right — not the first, skip it and take the next one.
Step 4
Drive All the Way to the End — Turn Right
Drive straight all the way to the end and turn right where the sign says Ramp Access to B2.
Step 5
Past the Ramp — Turn Left
Go past the ramp, continue all the way to the end and turn LEFT.
Step 6
Bay 217/43 — Closest to the Lift
Your car space is 217/43 — the bay closest to the lift door. There is only 1 space available. Pull in and the lift door is right next to your car.
Step 7
Take the Lift to Level 2
The lift door is right next to your parking space. Inside the lift, hold the remote above the button panel to activate it, then press Level 2.
Step 8
Find Apartment 217
Exit on Level 2 and head straight to the left at the end of the corridor. Apartment 217 is at the far end.
