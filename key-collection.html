📍 How to Find Your Keys

Step 1
Find the Colourful Mural
Head to Lonsdale Street, Braddon. Look for the large colourful rectangle mural on the side of the gold building. The arrow shows where to turn — turn left into the laneway next to Gusto's Pizza.
Step 2
Enter the Laneway
Once you've turned left into the laneway you'll see the lockboxes on the right hand side (indicated by the arrow). The lit up sign says Canstay.
Step 3
Collect Your Keys
Locate your lockbox number and enter the access code — both are visible in your reservation guide from 3:00 PM on check-in day. Take your keys, then close the lockbox and scramble the code.
⚠️
Don't have your lockbox number or code? Check your reservation guide which becomes available at 3:00 PM on check-in day. Still having trouble? Call us on 0493 173 122 — we respond within minutes.
